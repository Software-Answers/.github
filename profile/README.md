# Software Answers

> Long-term stay software for extended-stay hotels, serviced apartments, and corporate housing.

Welcome to the public GitHub presence for **Software Answers, Inc.**  
We build and maintain software products that power long-term stay operations end-to-end — from reservations and rate management to guest communication and back-office workflows.

We are the team behind **CodeOne** and **Oscar**.

---

## 💼 Who We Are

Software Answers is a **Long-Term Stay Software Provider** focused on:

- 🏨 Extended-stay hotels  
- 🏢 Corporate and project-based housing  
- 🏘️ Serviced apartments & other long-term accommodations  

Our goal is to help operators:

- Simplify daily operations  
- Improve guest experience over longer stays  
- Increase visibility into revenue and occupancy  
- Integrate cleanly with the tools they already use
