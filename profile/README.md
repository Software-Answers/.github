# Software Answers (Public GitHub)

**Software Answers builds the operational and financial backbone for long-term stay accommodation providers.**  
We create **property management systems (PMS)** purpose-built for the complexity of corporate housing, serviced apartments, relocation, and insurance lodging—where **operations and accounting must stay cleanly connected**.

---

## What we do

Long-term stay accommodations don’t run on chance — they run on systems that coordinate **inventory, reservations, turnover, billing, compliance, and financial controls** with consistency.

Software Answers builds the systems that **simplify the invisible complexity behind every long-term stay** and unite operations and accounting in a framework designed for **accuracy, scale, and trust**.

**We’re built for long-term stay — not hotels or vacation rentals.**  
That means software designed around leases, contract billing, vendor payables, month-long stays, client billing schedules, and audit-grade financial requirements.

---

## Who we serve

Our platform supports operators with multi-location execution, contract-driven billing, and finance-heavy environments where accuracy and control matter, including:

- Corporate housing providers  
- Serviced apartment operators  
- Relocation & insurance lodging (and adjacent long-stay segments)

## Products

### Oscar
**The operational and financial backbone for mature, multi-market long-term stay operators.**

Oscar is a modern, web-based PMS that unites daily operations and **ERP-grade accounting** in one platform — managing reservations, unit readiness, workflows, billing, and **audit-ready financials** without fragile handoffs.

Learn more: https://www.softwareanswers.com/products/oscar

### CodeOne
**Automation-first clarity for modern long-term stay operations.**

CodeOne is a modern SaaS platform for operators growing operators who’ve outgrown spreadsheets and lightweight tools — delivering faster, more accurate workflows and **trusted financials (without ERP overhead)**.

Learn more: https://www.softwareanswers.com/products/codeone
